About BMDC

#blackmendontcheat is a hashtag with increasing popularity which symbolizes that african american men don't have the capacity to cheat of their significant other. That may not neccessarily be true, but we should strive to make the hashtag a reality. Our objective is to give men a way to show their commitment to their significant other in a fun, loving way.
